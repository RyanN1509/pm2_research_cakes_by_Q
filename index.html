Competitive Analysis

Oakmont Bakery

oakmontbakery.com

Strengths

  • They have a clean logo and feature their tag line
  • Simple design easy to clients to use
  • A good use of photos of products.

Weaknesses

  • Could use better text it can be hard to read some of the information.
  • Some of the in page links are hard to realize there links to another page.

Confections By: Casey Renee

confectionspgh.com

Strengths

  • They use images frequently allowing users to see what they're getting.
  • Mobile friendly design.

Weaknesses

  • The company logo blends into the background image and tough to read.
  • Hero image takes up full page

The Cakery

thecakerypgh.com

Strengths

  • Strong visual identity.
  • Navbar only becomes a solid color after scrolling so you can see the background as a sort of hero image.

Weaknesses

  • pages with repeating elements make lists too long.
  • Elements don't go side by side.

Madison Ave Cakes

madisonavecakes.com

Strengths

  • The single page layout allows users to see everything at once.
  • Cool blue color scheme similar to what client would like.

Weaknesses

  • The single page may be too long, especially on mobile.
  • There is no full gallery, when you choose a cake category meaning users have to search through a disorganized gallery to see what they want.
